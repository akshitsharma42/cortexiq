# CortexIQ – Vision

## Problem

Organizations have knowledge scattered across:

- **PDFs** – Policy documents, research papers, contracts, reports
- **Meetings** – Decisions made verbally, action items lost in recordings
- **Repositories** – Code logic, architecture decisions, README files buried in branches
- **Documentation** – Wikis, Confluence pages, Notion docs, Google Docs
- **Chats** – Slack threads, Teams messages, email chains

Finding the right information at the right time is painful. Employees spend an average of **1.8 hours per day** searching for information. Knowledge leaves when people leave. Context is lost between teams.

**The cost of not knowing what your organization already knows is enormous.**

---

## Solution

**CortexIQ becomes the AI brain of an organization.**

It ingests, understands, and connects all organizational knowledge into a single intelligent layer. Instead of searching across 10 tools, you ask CortexIQ one question and get an answer — with citations, context, and confidence.

CortexIQ is not another chatbot. It is an **AI Operating System for knowledge**.

---

## Mission

> Ask anything. Get answers from your organizational knowledge instantly.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Accuracy over speed** – Every answer must be grounded in real documents. No hallucinations.
2. **Privacy first** – Your data stays yours. No training on customer data.
3. **Source transparency** – Every answer shows exactly where it came from.
4. **Progressive complexity** – Simple to start, powerful to scale.
5. **Team-native** – Built for collaboration, not just individual use.

| Segment | Pain Point | CortexIQ Value |
|---------|-----------|----------------|

| Mid-market (50-500) | Information silos between teams | Cross-team knowledge discovery |

## Long-term Vision

CortexIQ evolves from a Q&A tool into the **intelligent layer** of every organization — where meetings auto-summarize, code changes auto-document, and new employees get instant access to years of institutional knowledge.
